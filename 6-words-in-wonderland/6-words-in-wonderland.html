1. Enter a Start word and a Target word of equal length, then press Submit words.

2. If both words are valid, they're locked in place and the game begins.

3. Your goal is to change out one character at a time and still make a valid word every step of the way, from Start word to Target word.

3.1. Enter a word in Enter new word and press Try new word. If the word is valid and differs by exactly 1 character, the new word becomes your Current word

3.2. repeat 3.1 until Current word matches Target word Aim for as few steps as possible. Only valid words count as steps.
